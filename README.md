<!-- Features -->
### :dart: Features

- Create Account , Email Verification, Keeper Register , Login
- View Warehouse Products , View Warehouse Clients , View Warehouses , View Warehouse Details
- Add Warehouse , Add Client , Add Category , Add Product ,Add Export Order , Add Receive Order
- Receive Report , Sort Expiration date , Sort Quantity
- Search client by name , search client by location
- Search product by name , search product by category
- in view product ..border card has red color if Quantity in min or if product close to Expired
- in view client details or warehouse details ..user can click on number or email and will take him directly to phone or email box
- the application support arabic and english (localization)
